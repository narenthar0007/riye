About Champ Fitness

Welcome to **Champ Fitness**, a leading unisex gym dedicated to helping every individual achieve their health and fitness goals. We offer a comprehensive suite of services tailored to meet diverse needs, whether you're looking for personal transformation, specific strength gains, or overall wellness.

Our gym is founded and led by **Karthick Arasu**, a passionate fitness expert committed to creating a supportive and results-driven environment. We pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art equipment, experienced trainers, and a welcoming community that inspires greatness.

At Champ Fitness, we believe that fitness is a journey, not a destination. Let us be your partner in building a stronger, healthier, and more confident you.

Exercise Plan

Day 1: Chest, Shoulder, Cardio
Chest:
  • Barbell Bench Press (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
  • Incline Dumbbell Press (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
  • Cable Crossovers (3 sets x 10-15 reps)
Shoulder:
  • Overhead Dumbbell Press (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
  • Lateral Raises (3 sets x 10-15 reps)
  • Front Raises (3 sets x 10-15 reps)
Cardio:
  • Treadmill Run (15 minutes, moderate intensity)
  • Jump Rope (5 minutes)
  • Dynamic Stretching (5 minutes)
Day 2: Back, Cardio
Back:
  • Pull-ups / Lat Pulldowns (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
  • Barbell Rows (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
  • Seated Cable Rows (3 sets x 10-15 reps)
  • T-Bar Rows (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
  • Hyperextensions (3 sets x 10-15 reps)
  • Face Pulls (3 sets x 12-15 reps)
Cardio:
  • Elliptical (20 minutes, moderate intensity)
  • Core Plank (3 sets, hold 30-60 seconds)
  • Cool Down Stretch (5 minutes)
Day 3: Biceps, Triceps, Cardio
Biceps:
  • Barbell Curls (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
  • Incline Dumbbell Curls (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
  • Hammer Curls (3 sets x 10-15 reps)
Triceps:
  • Close-Grip Bench Press (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
  • Overhead Dumbbell Extension (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
  • Rope Pushdowns (3 sets x 10-15 reps)
Cardio:
  • Stationary Bike (15 minutes, steady pace)
  • Abdominal Crunches (3 sets x 15-20 reps)
  • Plank Variations (3 sets)
Day 4: Legs, Cardio
Quads:
  • Barbell Squats (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
  • Leg Press (3 sets x 10-15 reps)
Hamstrings:
  • Romanian Deadlifts (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
  • Leg Curls (3 sets x 10-15 reps)
Calves:
  • Standing Calf Raises (3 sets x 15-20 reps)
  • Seated Calf Raises (3 sets x 15-20 reps)
Cardio:
  • Incline Treadmill Walk (20 minutes)
  • Box Jumps (3 sets x 10 reps)
  • Cool Down Stretch (5 minutes)
