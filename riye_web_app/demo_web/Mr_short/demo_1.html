About Mr Shot

At Mr Shot, we believe that comfort shouldn't come at a high price. We are a pioneering water heater company dedicated to manufacturing and supplying instant water heaters that are both affordable and highly efficient. Our mission is to make warm water accessible to every household, ensuring quality without compromise.

With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and a commitment to innovation, we design our products to be durable, safe, and easy to use. We are proud to serve customers across Tamil Nadu and beyond, bringing a new standard of value to the instant water heater market. We are committed to sustainability and customer satisfaction.
