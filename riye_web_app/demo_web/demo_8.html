About Aqua Haven

Founded in 2010, Aqua Haven began as a small fish breeding operation and has grown into a premier destination for aquarium enthusiasts. Our passion for aquatic ecosystems drives everything we do.

We specialize in sustainable aquaculture, growing most of our fish and plants in-house to ensure quality and health. Our custom tank division has created stunning aquascapes for homes, offices, and commercial spaces throughout the region.

Whether you're a beginner looking for your first betta fish or an experienced hobbyist seeking rare species, our knowledgeable team is here to help you succeed in your aquatic journey.
